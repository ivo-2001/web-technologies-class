Sorrento

Sorrento, perched on the southwestern coast of Italy, serves as a delightful gateway to the Amalfi Coast. This charming town is known for its stunning views of the Bay of Naples, lemon groves, and an inviting old town filled with colorful buildings and lively streets. Sorrento is an excellent starting point for exploring the coastal gems, such as Positano and Amalfi, while offering its own unique blend of Italian charm, cuisine, and culture.
