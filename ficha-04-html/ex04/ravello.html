Ravello

Perched high above the Amalfi Coast, Ravello is a serene oasis known for its panoramic views, historic churches, and vibrant cultural scene. The town's famous gardens at Villa Cimbrone and Villa Rufolo have long inspired artists and travelers. Ravello's annual festival is a cultural highlight, while its tranquil ambiance and fewer tourists make it an ideal destination for a peaceful Italian escape. Whether wandering through its charming streets, exploring historic sites, or enjoying the views, Ravello offers an intimate encounter with the Amalfi Coast's magic.
