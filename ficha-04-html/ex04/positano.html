Positano

Positano, perched on the Amalfi Coast, is a captivating village known for its dramatic cliffs, colorful houses, and inviting beaches. The village's winding streets lead to boutique shops, cafes, and restaurants offering exceptional Italian cuisine. Rich in history, you can explore ancient watchtowers and visit the Church of Santa Maria Assunta.

Positano's relaxed atmosphere, stunning coastal views, and artistic heritage make it a haven for travelers in search of inspiration and natural beauty. A visit to Positano promises a memorable experience, whether for a day or an extended stay.
